NKDuy Scanner

Privacy policy

NKDuy Scanner does not collect any data from your device.

Permissions

  • 1. Camera: Allows the app to use camera to scan QR codes and barcodes.
  • 2. Read contacts: Allows the app to read contacts to create a QR code from a contact.
  • 3. Shared storage: Allows the app to access storage when you want to save your QR code or barcode as text or image.
  • 4. Connect and disconnect from Wi-Fi: Allows the app to connect to and disconnect from Wi-Fi access points and to make changes to device configuration for Wi-Fi networks.
  • 5. Control vibration: Allows the app to vibrate when a QR code or a barcode is scanned.

If you believe this policy has been violated, please let us know.
